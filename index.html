TECHNOLOGY

How It Works

01

Real Data (2016–2025)

9 seasons of Premier League data auto-downloaded from football-data.co.uk — 3420+ real matches with goals, shots, cards and more.

02

CSV Upload

Upload your own dataset in football-data.co.uk format. The model automatically retrains on your data and merges it with existing seasons.

03

Feature Engineering

21 features computed per fixture: form score (last 5), win/draw/loss rate, avg goals, goal diff, head-to-head record, home advantage.

04

Ensemble ML Model

Soft-voting ensemble of Random Forest (300 trees), Gradient Boosting (200 estimators) and Logistic Regression — trained fresh on each dataset.
